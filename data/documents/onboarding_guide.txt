# New Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to the Team!

Congratulations on joining our company! This guide will help you navigate your first weeks and get you up to speed with our processes, tools, and culture.

## First Day

### Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)
- **9:00 AM**: Arrive at reception, get your badge and welcome packet
- **9:30 AM**: Meet with HR for paperwork and benefits enrollment
- **10:30 AM**: IT setup - receive laptop, phone, and access credentials
- **11:00 AM**: Office tour and introductions to the team
- **11:45 AM**: Security and compliance training

### Afternoon (1 PM - 5 PM)
- **1:00 PM**: Lunch with your team
- **2:00 PM**: Meet with your manager for goal setting
- **3:00 PM**: Workstation setup and tool configuration
- **4:00 PM**: Review first week schedule and objectives
- **4:30 PM**: Q&A session with your onboarding buddy

## Company Culture

### Our Values
1. **Customer First**: Always prioritize customer needs
2. **Innovation**: Encourage new ideas and experimentation
3. **Collaboration**: Work together, help each other succeed
4. **Integrity**: Be honest, transparent, and ethical
5. **Excellence**: Strive for high-quality work

### Communication Norms
- Default to async communication when possible
- Use Slack for quick questions, email for formal requests
- Response time expectations: 4 hours during work hours
- Set status in Slack when unavailable
- Camera on for team meetings

### Meeting Culture
- All meetings have agendas
- Start and end on time
- Action items documented
- Decline meetings that don't need you
- "No meeting Fridays" for focus time

## Your First 30-60-90 Days

### Days 1-30: Learning Phase
**Goals**:
- Understand company culture and values
- Learn product and technology stack
- Build relationships with team
- Complete small, well-defined tasks
- Ask lots of questions

**Success Metrics**:
- Complete all onboarding training
- Ship first small contribution
- Understand team's sprint process
- Can navigate codebase independently

### Days 31-60: Contributing Phase
**Goals**:
- Take on larger tasks
- Participate in code reviews
- Contribute to team discussions
- Identify improvement opportunities
- Build cross-team relationships

**Success Metrics**:
- Deliver feature independently
- Provide value in sprint planning
- Mentor newer team members
- Understand broader product context

### Days 61-90: Ownership Phase
**Goals**:
- Own a project or feature area
- Mentor other developers
- Propose improvements
- Demonstrate company values
- Think strategically about work

**Success Metrics**:
- Lead a significant project
- Contribute to technical decisions
- Improve team processes
- Ready for full performance review

## Common Questions

### Q: When will I receive my equipment?
A: Laptop and phone issued on first day. Additional equipment requested through IT portal.

### Q: How do I submit expenses?
A: Use Expensify app. Upload receipts and submit for approval. Reimbursement within 2 weeks.

### Q: What's the dress code?
A: Business casual. Jeans okay. Professional for client meetings. Casual Fridays.

### Q: How does PTO work?
A: Request through BambooHR. Get manager approval 2 weeks in advance. Check team calendar for conflicts.

### Q: When is payday?
A: Bi-weekly on Fridays. First paycheck may take 2-3 weeks.
