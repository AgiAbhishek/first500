# Company Policies and Guidelines

## Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
- All full-time employees are entitled to 15 days of paid annual leave per year.
- Annual leave accrues at a rate of 1.25 days per month.
- Employees must submit leave requests at least 2 weeks in advance for planned vacations.
- Maximum rollover of 5 unused days to the next year.

### Sick Leave
- Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually.
- Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
- Sick leave does not roll over to the next year.

### Parental Leave
- Maternity leave: 16 weeks paid leave for birth mothers.
- Paternity leave: 4 weeks paid leave for fathers.
- Adoption leave: 12 weeks paid leave for adoptive parents.

## Work From Home Policy

- Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week.
- Must be approved by manager in advance.
- Employees must be available during core hours (10 AM - 4 PM).
- Required equipment: laptop, stable internet connection, dedicated workspace.

## Code of Conduct

### Professional Behavior
- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity.
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination.
- Maintain confidentiality of company and client information.

### Dress Code
- Business casual attire for office days.
- Professional appearance required for client meetings.
- Casual dress allowed on Fridays.

## Benefits

### Health Insurance
- Comprehensive health insurance for employees and dependents.
- Coverage includes medical, dental, and vision.
- Annual health checkup provided.

### Retirement Plan
- 401(k) with 5% company match.
- Vesting period: 3 years.
- Financial planning consultations available.

### Professional Development
- $2,000 annual learning budget per employee.
- Support for certifications and courses.
- Mentorship program available.

## Office Hours

- Standard office hours: 9 AM - 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Flexible hours available with manager approval.
- Core hours (all must be available): 10 AM - 4 PM.
